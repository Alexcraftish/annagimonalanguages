Services

Interpreting

Interpreting means rendering the meaning of an oral text from one language in another. We distinguish between simultaneous interpreting and consecutive interpreting. The first is carried out in simultaneous interpreting booths (known from the EU or UN) and as the name says – simultaneously – while the speaker is holding their speech. Consecutive interpreting takes place after the speaker finishes up (the interpreter will use note-taking techniques while listening to the speech).
I am a professional conference and community interpreter and my working languages are German, English and Spanish
Don’t hesitate to contact me and together we will find the best solution for your event. I work as an interpreter at international conferences and meetings, such as on tours through company buildings and areas. Likewise, I interpret in small meetings and get-togethers.

Translation

Translating is all about rendering the meaning of a written source-language text in a target language, taking into account cultural and linguistic aspects. I offer professional translations from the fields of medicine, economy, marketing, law, culture and more. Furthermore, you may commission translations of documents at Anna Gimona Languages. For a detailed quote, it is essential to review the material that needs to be translated, which you can send me via email. It goes without saying that I handle your documents with the utmost discretion.
